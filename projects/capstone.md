## 🌱 Sustainable Agribusiness Optimisation (NAB Industry Capstone)

### 🔍 Overview
Industry collaboration project with National Australia Bank (NAB), focused on designing a sustainable agribusiness solution using data-driven analysis and structured project management.

---

### 🎯 Key Contributions
- Designed a **biogas-based anaerobic digestion system** to reduce farm emissions
- Developed a full **Project Management Plan (PMP)** covering scope, budget, risks, and delivery
- Applied a **Waterfall methodology** across all project phases
- Built a **risk management framework (RPN scoring)** to prioritise project risks
- Created stakeholder models including **RACI matrix and Power–Interest Grid**

---

### ⚙️ Technical & Business Work
- Analysed environmental and operational data to support feasibility
- Estimated **energy generation, emissions reduction, and revenue streams**
- Developed detailed **project timeline, milestones, and deployment strategy**
- Ensured alignment with **EPA regulations and NAB green financing criteria**

---

### 📊 Key Outcomes
- Delivered a structured, real-world sustainability solution
- Demonstrated measurable environmental and economic impact
- Simulated real-world funding approval and stakeholder decision-making
